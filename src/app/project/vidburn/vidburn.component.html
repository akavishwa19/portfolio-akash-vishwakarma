VIDBURN :

demo

VidBurn is an AI-powered Node.js application that enhances your videos by automatically generating subtitles using OpenAI's Whisper and syncing relevant static images from Pexels. The final output is a video with burned-in subtitles and imagery that visually complements your content.

TECH : Angular 17 , Node JS , Python , Open AI Whisper , Ffmpeg

  • A server heavy application that accepts a video
  • Generates the subtitles and relevant images for the video using AI
  • Burns the subtitles and images on the original video perfectly synched
  • Return the AI transformed video to the user
